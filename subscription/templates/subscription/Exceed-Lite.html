Bloom UpLite

Being a budget hotel software, Bloom UpLite comes with 5 operationally essential modules. It is a set of fully integrated modules that handle reservation, room operations, housekeeping, guest engagement and so. This system removes all the concerns and expenses of purchasing the advanced hardware usually associated with property management systems.

Bloom UpHotel PMS perfectly suits the needs of various types of properties and used in hotels, motels, resorts, clubs, B & B's, small hotel franchisees, clubs, condos, hostels, apartments and more.
